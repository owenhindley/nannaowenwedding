Here's a moderately accurate map of the area so you can find your way around when you arrive.

The Camping areas are enormous, and extend way outside the area covered on this map, so if you're in a tent and want peace and quiet at night, you can find a nice spot in the woods (top of the map) or towards the big river (right of the map), where there's extra toilets and things.

We'll be spending most of the time in the decked area outside the Big and Small Cabins, where food will be served, and the party will party.
