Dress code is :
Glastonbury Festival, with UK October weather.

. . . . . . .

Meaning you can absolutely dress up - the more fabulous or outrageous the better for our part - but bear in mind that it may get a little cold.

Key items to have with you are:

  • fleece or wool jumper
  • thermal underwear
  • thick socks
  • gloves and hats
  • something to wear if it rains

So ditch the high heels in favour of a nice pair of boots or comfortable shoes, and Gore-Tex beats Brogues any day of the week. Hiking shoes or sturdy trainers are even better as we'll be going for a short walk up the mountain post-ceremony to take photos.

A suit, however, is still a suit if you're wearing a layer of thermals underneath (that's what Owen'll be wearing), and woollen jumpers with tights are a timeless Icelandic wedding look.
