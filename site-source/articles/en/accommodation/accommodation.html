Being a stunningly remote location in the Highlands, there are no 5-star hotels nearby.
You'll have three main options for accommodation:

A bed in one of the two cabins

Bring a 4x4 campervan or sturdy caravan

A tent!

We've booked out both cabins for the entire weekend, so you'll have access to everything mentioned below, even if you're not sleeping there.
