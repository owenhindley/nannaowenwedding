--- tags: - article - accommodation title: Tents id: section-tents ---

Tents are the simplest option, and camping in the woods at Þórsmörk is a great experience.

You can find a nice secluded spot in the forest next to a river (to keep the beer cool), or camp in one of the large, open spaces with benches and public grills. The area is large, but easy to navigate.

The campsite charges 2000 ISK per person per night for tents.
