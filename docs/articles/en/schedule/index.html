Friday 25th

  • Daytime

    People arrive in Þórsmörk, set up tents, get settled in the cabins, park their campervans, etc.

  • Evening

    Casual food (provided by us), drinks and kvöldvaka around the bonfire

Saturday 26th

  • Morning

    Breakfast (provided by us), appreciate the glorious nature, wedding couple get ready for ceremony.

  • 13.00-14.00ish

    Wedding ceremony in the campsite

  • Post-ceremony

    The couple, photographer, and whomever wants to join goes on a short hike up the mountain to take photos. Afternoon tea + light lunch for everyone else back at the campsite.

  • Early evening

    Couple returns from hike, buffet evening meal and drinks (provided by us)

  • Evening

    Party starts - live music and dancing, followed by DJs in the cabin until late.
